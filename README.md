## Proposed Solution
### 1. Detailed explanation of the proposed solution

The proposed solution is an AI-powered, multilingual Digital Biosecurity Farm Management Portal designed specifically for pig and poultry farmers. This platform will offer features such as risk assessment tools, farm biosecurity planning, interactive training modules, real-time disease alerts, and digital compliance tracking. It enables farmers to perform farm risk evaluation, generate customized biosecurity plans, track farm hygiene records, maintain visitor and disease logs, and access government-approved guidelines.<br>
The portal will also connect farmers with veterinarians, extension workers, and authorities through integrated communication channels, promoting collaborative disease control.
